# Sweet Potato Quesadillas


## Ingredients
<ul>
  <li>4 tortillas</li>
  <li>1 large sweet potato</li>
  <li>1 can red kidney beans</li>
  <li>1 bell pepper</li>
  <li>feta cheese</li>
  <li>salsa</li>
  <li>guacamole</li>
  <li>olive oil</li>
  <li>paprika</li>
  <li>cumin</li>
  <li>corriander</li>
  <li>mild chili powder</li>
  <li>salt</li>
  <li>pepper</li>
</ul>

### Optional Ingredients
<ul>
  <li>chorizo</li>
  <li>sour cream</li>
</ul>

## Method
<ol type="1">
  <li>Preheat the oven to 180 C (350 F)</li>
  <li>In a medium-sized pan, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil on medium heat</li>
  <li>Peel and chop the sweet potato into small chunks</li>
  <li>Either toss in olive oil and bake the sweet potato chunks in the oven on 180 C (350 F) for 35 minutes or until soft, or cook the chunks directly in the skillet</li>
  <li>Chop the bell pepper into small chunks</li>
  <li>Add the pepper to the pan to cook</li>
  <li>Add the paprika, cumin, corriander, mild chili powder, salt & pepper to the vegetables</li>
  <li>Cook the vegetables until soft</li>
  <li>Drain the water and add the red kidney beans to the pan with the vegetables and warm through (about 10 minutes)</li>
  <li>If using chorizo, cook the chorizo in a separate pan until crispy</li>
  <li>On a flat baking sheet, lay out the tortillas</li>
  <li>Spoon about 2 tablespoons of the vegetable/bean filling into each tortilla</li>
  <li>Crumble the feta into each tortilla</li>
  <li>Add chorizo to each tortilla</li>
  <li>Fold each tortilla perfectly in half</li>
  <li>Place the assembled tortillas in the oven for about 10 minutes, or until the outer edges of the tortilla wraps are golden</li>
  <li>Serve with salsa, guacamole, and sour cream</li>
</ol>
