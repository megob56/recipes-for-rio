# Rice and Bean Wraps

## Ingredients
<ul>
  <li>4 tortillas</li>
  <li>1 can chopped tomatos</li>
  <li>1 can black beans</li>
  <li>2 bell peppers</li>
  <li>2 cups cooked rice</li>
  <li>olive oil</li>
  <li>paprika</li>
  <li>cumin</li>
  <li>corriander</li>
  <li>mild chili powder</li>
  <li>salt</li>
  <li>pepper</li>
  <li>1 avocado</li>
</ul>

## Method

<ol type="1">
  <li>In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil on medium heat</li>
  <li>Finely chop the bell peppers and cook them in the pan until soft (approximately 10 minutes)</li>
  <li>Add the can of chopped tomatoes to the pan</li>
  <li>Drain the canned black beans and add them as well</li>
  <li>Add the paprika, cumin, corriander, mild chili powder, salt & pepper to the pan</li>
  <li>Cook on medium heat until reduced slightly and not so watery</li>
  <li>Chop the avocado</li>
  <li>Spoon about 2 tablespoons of the tomato/bean filling and 2 tablespoons of the cooked rice into a tortilla</li>
  <li>Add the avocado into the tortilla wrap as well</li>
  <li>Roll up and eat!</li>
</ol>
