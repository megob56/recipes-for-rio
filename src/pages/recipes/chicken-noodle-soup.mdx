# Chicken Noodle Soup

## Ingredients

<ul>
  <li>Cooked chicken breast</li>
  <li>olive oil</li>
  <li>4 large carrots</li>
  <li>3 celery stalks</li>
  <li>1 chicken stock cube</li>
  <li>8 cups water</li>
  <li>1 cup pasta</li>
  <li>1 cup spinach</li>
  <li>a pinch of chili flakes</li>
  <li>mixed herbs</li>
  <li>salt</li>
  <li>pepper</li>
</ul>

## Method

<ol type="1">
  <li>Fill the crockpot with 8 cups of water and turn on high heat</li>
  <li>Add the stock cube to the water</li>
  <li>Chop the carrots and celery into small, thin slices/chunks</li>
  <li>On the stove, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a medium-sized frying pan on medium heat</li>
  <li>Cook the carrots and celery in the pan until soft (~10-15 minutes)</li>
  <li>Pour the cooked vegetables into the crockpot</li>
  <li>Add a pinch of chili flakes, mixed herbs and salt & pepper to taste</li>
  <li>After 3 hours has passed, add the pasta to the crockpot</li>
  <li>Let cook for another 30 minutes and then add the spinach</li>
  <li>Once 4 hours has passed, the soup should be ready</li>
</ol>
