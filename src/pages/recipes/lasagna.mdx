# Lasagna

## Ingredients

<ul>
  <li>4 large carrots</li>
  <li>2 cloves garlic</li>
  <li>1 can chopped tomatos</li>
  <li>1 jar tomato passata</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons tomato paste</li>
  <li>1 beef stock cube</li>
  <li>1 packet minced beef</li>
  <li>1 & 1/2 tubs ricotta</li>
  <li>1/2 cup shredded mozarella</li>
  <li>olive oil</li>
  <li>mixed herbs</li>
  <li>salt</li>
  <li>pepper</li>
</ul>

## Method

1. Preheat the oven to 180 C (350 F)
2. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large pot on *medium* heat 
3. Finely chop the garlic
4. Chop the carrots into thin, round slices
5. Add the garlic to the pot
6. Once the garlic has become a light golden color, add the carrots
7. Cook the carrots until soft (about 5-8 minutes)
8. Add the ground beef and cook until browned
9. Add the can of chopped tomatos, the jar of passata and the tomato paste to the pot
10. Add salt & pepper to taste
11. Add the mixed herbs and stir
12. Let simmer for about 20 minutes and stir occasionally to keep from sticking to bottom of the pan
13.  In a 9 x 13 baking dish, scoop out 2 cups of the meat mixture into the bottom of the dish
14. Layer lasagna pasta sheets on top of the meat mixture
15. Add another 2 cups of meat mixture on top of the pasta and spread evenly
16. Use a spoon to scoop spoonfuls of ricotta on top of the meat mixture 
17. Add another layer of pasta on top of the ricotta
18. Repeat the layering process
19. Pour the remaining meat sauce over the final layer of lasagna pasta
20. Top with mozarella cheese
21. Bake in the oven on the middle / bottom rack for 20-25 minutes until top is golden and bubbling
